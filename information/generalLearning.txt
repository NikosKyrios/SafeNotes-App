
--------------------
Q: What does initModality(Modality.APPLICATION_MODAL) do?
A: Creates a blocking popup window. Users must close it before interacting with any other part of the application. Used for important dialogs like login, error messages, and security prompts.
--------------------

Q: Why use System.getProperty("user.home") for file paths?
A: Cross-platform compatibility:

Windows: C:\Users\Username

macOS: /Users/Username

Linux: /home/username

This ensures the app works correctly on all operating systems without manual configuration.
